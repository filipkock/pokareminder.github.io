5. Retention

  • Medication data deleted upon request or account deletion
  • Payment metadata retained per tax requirements

5a. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App under Settings → Account → Delete account. When initiated, the App deletes your personal data stored in our systems that we control, including:

  • Account/profile document (user settings and identifiers)
  • Medication, reminders, streaks and reward cards data
  • Associated application data stored in our database (Firestore)

What is not cancelled or erased by us: Deleting your account does not cancel your App Store or Google Play subscription. To stop future charges, manage your subscription in your store account settings (e.g., App Store Subscriptions).

What we may retain: Certain records may be retained where required by law (e.g., tax/financial record‑keeping) and non‑identifying, aggregated analytics. Platform receipts and transaction records processed by Apple/Google are maintained by those platforms in accordance with their own policies.

We aim to process in‑app deletion requests immediately. Backup systems and logs are cycled and purged on a scheduled basis. If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, contact us at pokareminder@gmail.com.

6. Sharing & Processors

We never sell your data. Operational providers:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – authentication, database (Firestore), and cloud messaging (push tokens) (Standard Contractual Clauses)
  • RevenueCat, Inc. – subscription handling
  • Expo (EAS Build) – build infrastructure (no direct user data)
  • Apple / Google – payment processing (no direct card data stored by us)

6a. No Advertising or Tracking

We do not use third‑party advertising SDKs and we do not track you across apps or websites. We do not sell your personal data.
