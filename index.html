Who is Poka

Poka is 87% water, 13% penguin, 0% okay with you forgetting your meds.

Personality

Fun fact

When stressed, Poka can accidentally melt into a small puddle but don’t worry, he eventually re-forms… usually.

The App

Poka is your friendly medication side‑quest. Get gentle nudges when it’s time, check off your dose, and watch Poka relax. As you stay consistent you level up, unlock collectible cards, and build a deck that shows your streak power. Miss a day? Poka gets a bit melty, jump back in and rescue him.
